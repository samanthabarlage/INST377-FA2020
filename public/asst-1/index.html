Horoscope Signs

Individuals have different characteristics that are based on their zodiacs. There are different categories for each sign such as Fire, Air, Earth, and Water signs.

  1. Air Sign

    Air signs tend to be social individuals and very rational.

    • Gemini
    • Libra
    • Aquarius

  2. Water Signs

    Individuals under this sign tend to be very emotional.

    • Cancer
    • Pisces
    • Scorpio

  3. Fire Signs

    People under this sign tend to be passionate individuals who sometimes tend to have temper issues.

    • Aries
    • Sagitarrius
    • Leo
